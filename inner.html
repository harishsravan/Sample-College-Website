New York Institute of Technology

The New York Institute of Technology (NYIT or New York Tech) is a private research university founded in 1955. It has two main campuses in New Yorkâ€”one in Old Westbury, on Long Island, and one in Manhattan. Additionally, it has a cybersecurity research lab, a biosciences, bioengineering lab and the Entrepreneurship and Technology Innovation Center which has close links to NASA, in Old Westbury,[10][11] as well as campuses in Arkansas, China, and Canada. Our Facilites In NYIT

World Class Library

Every section of the library is a curated journey, each aisle a passage to a new realm of understanding. From the sciences to the arts, philosophy to pop culture, the World Class Library houses an extensive collection that spans the entirety of human knowledge.

First Class Hostels

You can expect spacious and well-designed dormitories or private rooms with comfortable beds, clean linens, and often en-suite bathrooms. The interiors are usually tastefully decorated, creating a welcoming and vibrant ambiance.

Largest Play Ground

The largest playground you've ever seen, a sprawling expanse of joy and excitement. Towering swings reach for the sky, their seats inviting you to soar to new heights. Colorful slides twist and turn, promising thrilling descents that leave you breathless with laughter.
